Hackathon Theme
Real-World Financial Solutions for Filipinos
Build solutions that improve how money moves, grows, and is accessed — for OFWs, MSMEs, unbanked communities, and everyday Filipinos.
🔑 Focus Areas
Choose one or combine multiple tracks:
💸 Payments & Remittances — Cheaper, faster money transfers for OFWs and families
🌏 Financial Inclusion — Wallets and tools for the unbanked
🪙 Stablecoins & PayFi — Programmable payments and payment finance solutions
🏪 MSME & Commerce Tools — Invoicing, payroll, and cashflow for small businesses
🛠️ Developer Tools on Stellar — SDKs, infrastructure, and utilities for the ecosystem
